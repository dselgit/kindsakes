How it works

A gentle, three-step flow that makes it easy to give and keep notes of kindness.

Step 1

Write your note

Say a few heartfelt lines. Add a photo if you like.

Step 2

We deliver it

Your recipient gets a private link via email or text.

Step 3

It’s kept safe

All your sent and received notes live in one place.

What happens next?

  • • Recipients can reply with a note of their own.
  • • You can group notes into bundles (e.g., birthday, graduation).
  • • You can download/print any note anytime.

Privacy, simply

Notes are private by default. Share a note or bundle with a link only when you choose.
