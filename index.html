How it works

Step 1

Write your note

Say the thing you mean—short and heartfelt. Add a photo if you like.

Step 2

We deliver it

Your recipient gets a private link in their email or text—no account required.

Step 3

It’s kept safe

All your sent and received notes live in one place, easy to revisit anytime.

Why it matters

  • Words outlast moments. Make them easy to keep.
  • Private by default, shareable when you choose.
  • Designed to feel like a gift, not an app.

A few ways people use Kindsakes

Milestone bundles

Collect 10 notes for a birthday, graduation, or first day as a manager.

Everyday encouragement

Send a quick “you’ve got this”—tiny notes add up over time.

Thank-a-teacher

Invite a class to write a few lines that a teacher can revisit forever.
